Joey Tianyi Zhou

I am a senior scientist, PI with the A*STAR Centre for Frontier AI Research (CFAR), Singapore.
I also hold an appointment as an Adjunct faculty at the National University of Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), SG, in 2015. I had been a snr. research engineer at Sony US Research Center, San Jose, USA before.

My research focus is on how to enhance Machine Learning Efficiency and Robustness. An ideal machine learning model shall : (1) be resource-efficient for both inference and training, including computational-efficient and data-efficientbe; (2) robust to various perturbations, distribution shifts, and adversarial attacks.
