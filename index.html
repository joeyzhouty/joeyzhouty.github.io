Brief Bio:

Hi! My name is Joey Tianyi Zhou. I am a senior scientist, PI, and group manager with the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), A*STAR, Singapore. I am leading the AI Group of more than 30 research staff members. I also hold an appointment as an Adjunct Assistant Professor at the National University of Singapore. Earlier, I worked as a Senior Research Engineer with Sony US Research Center, San Jose, USA. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), SG, in 2015. My research topics focus on Machine Learning with Limited Resources of data and hardware. and its applications.
